Instructions: Record your voice transaction

The recorded voice should be in this format "Bought item cost" or "Sold item cost" For example you can say "Bought sugar 200" if you did a purchase or "Sold sugar 200" if you did a sale of sugar Your purchases are saved as your expenses as they are cost you incurr and your sales are the revenue generated
Note: Please adhere to this format for your transaction to be successfully recorded in the system.
On the next page you have to click on a red button to start recording, The button will turn amber after clicking on it to show that you are recording. After that click on stop button to stop recording.

After you are done recording the trascription of your voice will be displayed to you on the text box written "Trascribed Text here."
The system will handle the rest of your transaction storage for you.
You can now check the dashboard to see the changes based on your recorded transaction. You can decide to download the report for you expense, revenue or income statement for a specific date (s) of your choice. It is that easy. Enjoy.
